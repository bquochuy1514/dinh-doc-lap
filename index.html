About the Independence Palace

The Independence Palace is a symbol of Vietnam's struggle for independence and freedom. It is a reminder of the sacrifices made by the Vietnamese people to achieve independence. The palace stands as a historical landmark that witnessed many significant events during the Vietnam War and continues to educate visitors about the country's rich history.

Independence Palace

The Independence Palace is a unique architectural masterpiece in Ho Chi Minh City, built between 1962 and 1966. This site is not only an important historical landmark but also an attractive destination that draws thousands of visitors each year.
